Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

Get quick answers to your questions about our dog walking and training services in Perth

What's included in the Dog Squad Adventures?

Our Dog Squad Adventures include:

  • 90-minute group walks in exciting locations around Perth
  • Convenient pick-up and drop-off service
  • Professional photos shared via our app
  • Small groups (maximum 4-5 dogs) for personalized attention
  • Free meet & greet session to ensure it's a good fit

All adventures are led by certified dog handlers who are trained in pet first aid.

How do Solo Adventures differ from group walks?

Solo Adventures are perfect for dogs who prefer one-on-one attention or need special care. These sessions include:

  • Personalized 60-minute walks tailored to your dog's pace
  • Flexible scheduling to suit your routine
  • Direct home pick-up and drop-off
  • Regular updates and photos during the walk
  • Optional training reinforcement during walks

How do I schedule a meet & greet session?

Scheduling a meet & greet is easy:

  1. Click the 'Book Adventure' or 'Schedule Walk' button
  2. Fill out our quick registration form
  3. Select 'Meet & Greet' as your service type
  4. Choose a convenient time slot
  5. We'll confirm within 24 hours

Meet & greets are free and typically last 30 minutes, allowing us to assess your dog's needs and answer any questions.

What safety measures do you have in place?

Your dog's safety is our top priority. Our safety measures include:

  • All staff are certified in pet first aid
  • Comprehensive insurance coverage
  • GPS tracking during walks
  • Small group sizes (maximum 4-5 dogs)
  • Thorough assessment of group dynamics
  • Regular staff training updates
  • Emergency protocols in place

What does the Walk & Train service include?

Our Walk & Train service combines exercise with professional training:

  • Initial consultation to assess needs and goals
  • Customized training plan development
  • 60-minute sessions combining walking and training
  • Progress tracking and regular updates
  • Take-home exercises and training tips
  • Focus on positive reinforcement techniques

This service is perfect for dogs who need extra guidance while getting their exercise.

How do I join the Holiday Boarding waitlist?

To join our Holiday Boarding waitlist:

  1. Click the 'Join Waitlist' button on our website
  2. Complete the boarding request form
  3. Specify your desired dates
  4. Submit your details

We'll contact you as soon as space becomes available. Priority is given to regular clients and early bookings.
