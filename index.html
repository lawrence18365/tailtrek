Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What's included in the Dog Squad Adventures?

Our Dog Squad Adventures include:

  • 90-minute group walks in exciting locations
  • Convenient pick-up and drop-off service
  • Professional photos shared via our app
  • Small groups for personalized attention

How do I book a Dog Squad Adventure?

Booking a Dog Squad Adventure is easy:

  • Visit our website and navigate to the "Book Now" section.
  • Select your preferred date and time.
  • Provide your dog's details and any special requirements.
  • Complete the payment process to confirm your booking.

What should I bring for my dog's adventure?

For a smooth experience, please bring the following:

  • Your dog's leash and collar.
  • Any necessary medications or special food.
  • A water bottle for your dog.
  • Proof of up-to-date vaccinations.

What safety measures are in place?

Your dog's safety is our top priority. We ensure:

  • All dogs are supervised by trained professionals.
  • Small group sizes for better control and attention.
  • Regular health and safety checks during the adventure.
  • Emergency protocols are in place at all times.

Can I cancel or reschedule my booking?

Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your booking:

  • Cancellations made 48 hours before the adventure are fully refundable.
  • Rescheduling is free if done at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Contact our support team for assistance with cancellations or rescheduling.

What if my dog has special needs?

We accommodate dogs with special needs:

  • Please inform us of any medical conditions or behavioral issues during booking.
  • Our team will tailor the adventure to suit your dog's requirements.
  • We have trained staff to handle special cases with care and expertise.
