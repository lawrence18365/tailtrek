Meet Codie

Codie's passion for animals began with her first dog, Ben, a Golden Labrador who was her loyal companion for 13 years. She started her career while completing a Certificate III in CompanionAnimal Studies and later earned her Certificate IV in Veterinary Nursing.

With over a decade of experience managing a WA family-owned pet shop, Codie specialized in canine nutrition, behavior, and product selection. Now a dedicated dog trainer, she combines her extensive knowledge and experience to help pet owners build positive relationships with their dogs.

Meet Teila

Teila's lifelong passion for animals began in her youth, spending school holidays working with horses, exploring the ocean through snorkelling and diving, and taking her Ridgeback on countless adventures. Her love for marine life led her to earn a diploma in Marine Science while also working at a local aquarium.

As one of Kalgoorlie's most sought-after foster carers, she dedicated several years to rescuing and rehabilitating animals, caring for more than 200 dogs and countless litters of puppies and kittens. Her commitment to animal welfare led her to earn Municipal Law A & B, ranger certifications, and wildlife carer's certificates.

Certified Wildlife Carer
Animal Rehabilitation Expert
